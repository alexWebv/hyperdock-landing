Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

What we collect

  • Account data (email, name, subscription tier).
  • App telemetry (crash logs, basic diagnostics). No command contents are sent unless you opt in.
  • Voice & AI: if cloud processing is enabled, audio/transcripts and prompts may be sent to chosen providers to return results.
  • Payment data is handled by our payment processor; we store limited billing metadata.

How we use data

  • Provide and improve the App and Site.
  • Process subscriptions and support.
  • Secure the service and prevent abuse.

Your choices

  • Use local speech recognition where available.
  • Disable cloud AI features at any time.
  • Access, edit, or delete your account data by contacting us.

Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law. You can request deletion of your account data.

Legal basis (EEA/UK)

We rely on performance of a contract, legitimate interests (e.g., service security), and consent (for optional features like cloud AI).

International transfers

Data may be processed in other countries. We use appropriate safeguards where required.

Children

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required by your jurisdiction).

Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes

We will post updates to this page and revise the “Last updated” date.
